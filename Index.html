About CodeConflux

CodeConflux is an annual technical fest bringing together students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts. With hands-on workshops, exciting competitions, and networking opportunities, CodeConflux is your chance to engage with the best in technology and innovation.

What to Expect

  • Participate in technical workshops led by industry experts
  • Join challenging competitions to showcase your skills
  • Network with peers and professionals in various tech fields
  • Win prizes, certificates, and exclusive CodeConflux merchandise!

Event Schedule

  • Day 2: Competitions and Panel Discussions
  • Day 3: Networking Sessions and Awards Ceremony
